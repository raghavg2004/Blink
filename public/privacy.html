Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Blink, we may collect:

  • Video/Audio Data: Your video and audio streams during active sessions (not stored)
  • Connection Data: IP addresses, device information, and connection timestamps
  • Optional Interests: Tags you provide for matching purposes
  • Reports: Information submitted when reporting another user

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate real-time video connections
  • Improve service quality and prevent abuse
  • Respond to support requests and reports
  • Analyze usage patterns (aggregated data only)

3. Data Retention

We do not store your video/audio streams after sessions end. Connection logs are retained for 30 days for moderation purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • STUN/TURN servers for connectivity (may see your IP)
  • Analytics services (anonymous usage data only)

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of data collection (may limit functionality)

6. Children's Privacy

Blink is not for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
